How to read this board

The Quality Risk Index prioritises who to have a supervision conversation with first — it is not an automated performance judgement. Rates are normalised per 100 completed visits so busier staff are not penalised, conscientious frequent reporters with strong feedback are flagged as a Strong reporter rather than a risk, and RAG colouring is withheld where the sample is too small to be fair (fewer than 50 completed visits or 10 feedback responses).
